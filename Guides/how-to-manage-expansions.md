#### How to manage the expansions

1. Open and edit ``yourdomain.com/launcher/Config.xml`` and look for examples in the ``<Expansions>`` section:


```xml
<!-- 
    EXPANSION IDS:
    1 = Classic
    2 = Burning Crusade
    3 = Wrath of the Lich King
    4 = Cataclysm
    5 = Mists of Pandaria
    6 = Warlords of Draenor
    7 = Legion
    8 = Battle for Azeroth
    9 = Shadowlands

    MENU ICON IDS:
    1 = Chat Bubble
    2 = Exclamation Star
    3 = Shopping Cart
    4 = Download Icon
-->
```
